About This Website

Tekken Archive is a fan-made website created to collect and organize everything related to the Tekken series. The goal is to provide easy access to game information, history, and downloadable content for fans of the franchise.

What You Can Find

  • 🎮 All Tekken games (Arcade & Console)
  • 🎬 Movies and animated series
  • ⭐ Extras like mods, music, and wallpapers

Purpose

This website is built for educational and entertainment purposes. It helps fans explore the Tekken universe in one place with a clean and modern interface.

Contact

You can improve this website by adding more content and features. This is an evolving project made by a Tekken fan.
